         Web-Application has three layers
  
         1)Front-End/User Interface : 
                                  Create using html,css,java,react 
                The Front Developer is also called Front end developer
           
         2)Back-End:
               Brain of your web App
               Logic of your website
           Those who know backend are called Back-End Developer

          3)DB Layer
             Store & Maintain Data
            
       
    The Engineer who can work on these layer is known as Full Stack Web Developer
